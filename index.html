about me

Hey! My name is Christiana. I'm a designer and illustrator from Southern California living in The Netherlands.

My love of art started in childhood, with my favorite activity being making silly drawings. This love continues to this day.

I have a wide range of creative styles, which can be a blessing and a curse (as I can't stick with just one). You can see the variety of aesthetics in my portfolio, which I attempted to categorize.

I make photorealistic portraits of people and pets, stylized characters/environments, and bright graphics for souvenirs.

Please don't hesitate to contact me for any inquiries, I'd be happy to get in contact.

With love,
Christi
